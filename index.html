The Early Game

Welcome to TPT2 i guess ðŸ‘‹

Throughout the entire game your primary source of progression is through the headquarters building, this being to increase your military tier.

Keep doing runs, despite how short they are to farm town resources . Then inside of the Construction Firm convert enough town resources to buy the Workshop

After each run, go to the workshop, and convert your white resources (Town Resources) into blue resources . How you want to spend them is preference, but i usualy go for damage and health on a new save, and invest in attack speed when you can afford it. Each level in here will push back the cost scaling in run, allowing you to scale much harder for cheaper.

Each time you level up you will gain a skill point. For your first skillpoints i would suggest recycling and engineering inside of the workshop

Your goal is to keep doing this untill you unlock 3 key modules that will carry the early game:

multishot

basic bounce

natures touch

Once you have obtained those 3 you essentialy become a G O D

Keep doing runs, converting resources -> , and upgrading multishot and basic bounce untill about 20% each.
Note: You do NOT need to level natures touch yet, this is essentially only used for the heal right now. Once you have both modules at around 20%, you can now remove regen from your blueprint as NT will carry your healing. Next step, attack speed and damage!

At this point, you can also start holding W to skip waves as soon as possible.

Inside of the headquarters there is a tab for contracts, these contracts allow you to gain resource bonuses by having your blueprint meet certain criteria. The only important one right now is glass cannon as this can easily be activated, by simply having only 1 defensive module: Stone foundation. As NT is our primary source of healing. Make sure to keep renewing this contract once every 10 minutes!

Round 100

Around this point you should be nearing round 100, Congrats! This will be your first milestone in TPT2 as it allows you to begin increasing your military tier.
Before doing so make sure to activate the longest glass cannon contract possible in the headquaters, this will keep running when you tier up, despite most other things being reset!
